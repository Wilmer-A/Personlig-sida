Formula 1 2020 Statistics Archive

Formula 1 2020 Statistics Archive is a website where I store information about the 2020 Formula 1 season. It can be used to for example learn some of the most important things to know if you are new to Formula 1, or to check where and when the next race is. Currently there are three tables where you can find information about the race calendar, which teams are competing and which tyres are used.
